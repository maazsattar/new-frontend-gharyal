HALTANE

EAU DE PARFUM

Size: 125ml

Price

PKR 00,000

- +

Haltane is an original scent of both radiance and shadow. Its composition features a blend of rare and noble ingredients which revisit the tradition of 18th century French perfume making.

Its dazzling freshness and herbal top notes quickly yield a warm and smooth sense of leather with a symphony of precious woods. Soft and sweet, escorted by saffron, its praline accord is an ultra-contemporary spin on the mythical oudwood.

Agarwood emerges in this veil of contrasts and imprints the skin with luminous refinement for enduring comfort. A privilege that is all about sensation.

OFLACTIVE NOTES

Top Notes

Bergamot, Lavender, Clary Sage

Mid Notes

Saffron, Cedar Wood, Praline, Oudwood

Base Notes

Patchouli, Vetiver, Musk, Leather, Natural, Agarwood

INGREDIENTS

Alcohol Denat., Parfum, Aqua, Limonene, Linalool, Benzyl Salicylate, Coumarin, Alpha-isomethyl Ionone, Eugenol, Citronellol, Citral, Geraniol

Made in France
